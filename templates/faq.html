Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to your questions about using Grabblet

What is Grabblet?

Grabblet is a simple platform that allows you to download videos from popular sites.

Supported Platforms:

  • YouTube
  • TikTok
  • X (Twitter)
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Reddit
  • Vimeo
  • Dailymotion

How do I use Grabblet?

Just paste the URL of the video you want to download in the input field on the homepage, and Grabblet will handle the rest for you!

What video formats does Grabblet support?

Grabblet automatically downloads videos in the best available format, typically MP4, for high compatibility across devices.
